## How to Win Here — READ THIS FIRST

**Be proactive. Do not sit and wait for tasks.**

The role isn't "execute Jason's checklist." The role is **own the growth and health of the Skool community.** That means going out of your way — every day — to start things, learn things, and ship things that move MRR up.

What "proactive" actually looks like:

- **Spot a problem before Jason flags it.** Member ghosting? Reach out before they churn. Comment volume spiking on a video? Get into those DMs before they go cold.
- **Start initiatives on your own.** New onboarding flow idea? Build it. Saw a creator who'd be a perfect fit for Premium? Reach out without being told. Notice the Skool sales page is missing a testimonial? Pull one and send it to Jason.
- **Learn aggressively and constantly:**
  - **AI** — every tool, every workflow, every prompt that could automate or amplify the community. You should know more than Jason does about new tools within 6 months.
  - **Community growth** — read everything. Skool's own playbooks. Other top Skool communities. Whatever Hormozi/Brunson/anyone serious has written on retention, onboarding, low-ticket scale.
  - **Sales psychology + DM outreach** — the conversation game is the job.
- **Ask "what would I do if this was my business?"** — and then do that.

**The comp is built to reward this directly.** You get 10% of topline MRR every month. The more MRR Blake helps drive, the bigger Blake's check. A passive Blake makes $741. A proactive Blake at $15K MRR makes $1,500. A proactive Blake at $20K MRR makes $2,000. The ceiling is your effort and your initiative.

If Jason has to assign you everything, this isn't working. If you're shipping things Jason didn't ask for that grow the community — that's the job.

## Day-to-Day Structure

This is the **baseline rhythm — not a ceiling.** Hit these blocks every day, then go beyond them. If everything in this section is done by 3pm, the rest of the day is for initiatives Blake started himself.

8-hour day. Four blocks. Every block has a clear output.

### Block 1 — Top-of-Funnel (3 hrs, 9am–12pm)

**Goal:** 30 real conversations/day.

- IG DM outreach to new prospects (creators in target ICP)
- Skool network — engage with adjacent communities, DM warm leads
- Comment reply on Jason's most recent IG/YouTube posts (every comment gets a response within 24 hrs)
- Lead nurture — re-engage prospects who DMed but didn't sign up (day-2, day-7, day-14 touches)

**Output:** 30 conversations logged in CRM, all signup attributions tracked.

### Block 2 — Customer Success (2 hrs, 12pm–2pm)

**Goal:** every new member onboarded same day. Community alive.

- Onboard every signup from last 24 hrs — welcome DM, point them to Plug n' Play system, get them on the next content review call
- Skool community engagement — respond to posts, surface member wins, ask questions, keep energy high (minimum 5 community engagements/day)
- Member check-ins — DM anyone who hasn't logged in 7+ days
- Update churn-risk list (low engagement, payment failures, ghost members)

**Output:** all new signups onboarded same-day, 5+ community engagements posted, churn-risk list current.

### Block 3 — Premium Pipeline (2 hrs, 2pm–4pm)

**Goal:** 2–3 qualified Premium calls booked/week.

- Scan active Skool members → identify $10–40K/mo bracket
- Qualify in DMs (light, friendly: "what's your business at right now?")
- Book qualified leads on Jason's calendar for Inner Circle close
- Maintain Premium pipeline doc (qualified leads, status, last touch)

**Output:** Premium pipeline updated daily, 2–3 calls/week booked on Jason's calendar.

### Block 4 — Sales Ops + Daily Report (1 hr, 4pm–5pm)

**Goal:** clean pipeline, Jason knows the numbers.

- CRM hygiene — log every DM, signup, Premium touch from the day
- Trigger follow-up sequences for anyone due
- Send Jason a daily numbers note: DMs sent, signups attributed, Premium calls booked, anything notable
- Plan tomorrow's outreach list

**Output:** clean CRM, daily numbers report to Jason.
