# Vision

**Status:** LOCKED 2026-05-03

## Mission
Make quality online education around AI, content, and online business actually **accessible** — not gatekept behind $5K–$50K mentor programs.

The high-ticket info space is mostly LARP. Build something real and genuine that helps a lot of people, not a small handful at a premium price.

## The real reason
Build a future for myself. Work for myself. Make enough to comfortably provide for my future wife and kids. Live the life I want — not anyone else's version of "success."

## Why this exists (the pivot)
- Spent years flip-flopping between business models (content coaching → affiliate → B2C → B2B → low-ticket → back again)
- High-ticket grind required reps with low-quality prospects + constant mentor-program purchases that didn't move the needle
- The high-ticket coaching space is mostly overcharging — not a model worth building
- April 2026 missed the $40K target. Cash brought in was mostly baseline. Pivot needed.

## Current state (May 2026)
- **$7,410/mo Skool MRR** · 173 members · 173k IG · 9k YT — slowly declining
- 6 high-ticket clients finishing out, not being replaced — that revenue rolls off
- Poppy affiliate, brand deals — supplemental, sporadic, not bankable
- **Translation:** if Skool MRR doesn't grow, total income shrinks fast

## Consequences if nothing changes
- Years pass without compounding growth on a single motion
- Energy leak chasing high-ticket closes that produce traps more than revenue
- Skool stays sub-$10K MRR forever because it's never been the main thing
- Peer gap with creators who picked one motion and ran it stays uncloseable

## Desired state
- **One motion, 18 months minimum, no flip-flopping.** That motion is Skool.
- Hundreds of low-ticket members compounding on retention + content
- Premium tier is the natural upsell for members who want more
- Team handles fulfillment / sales / ops; I work on content + vision
- 4–5 content pieces shipping every week, no excuses
- Income converts into assets — investments, properties

## MRR ladder
- **$20K Skool MRR** = mission-critical, near-term — get back to where we should be
- **$50K Skool MRR** = long-term ceiling for the low-ticket motion. Others have done it; nothing about $50K is unrealistic, it's just not the focus *right now*.
- Order matters: $20K first (rebuild + retention), then push for $50K. Don't skip steps.

## Backstory — how I got here

### Past situation
Hit a low point in college. Looked around and realized everyone was sleepwalking — following the script, settling for average. Refused to follow that path. Dropped out, went all in on online business. No advantages. No family money, no connections. Started from $0. Spent $30K+ on education. Got burned by scammy courses. Every failure taught me what NOT to do.

### Past pains
Felt broke, felt invisible, felt like a nobody. Believed if I didn't change things, my life would be average — maybe even struggling. I wanted more.

### Past desires
An audience. A way to monetize my passion. Time and location freedom. Get paid to exist.

### Epiphany
Invested in real mentorship from people who were already where I wanted to be. That's when it clicked. Hit my first $10K/mo and never looked back.
