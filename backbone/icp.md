# ICP — Ideal Customer Profile

**Status:** LOCKED 2026-05-03

Two tiers, two audiences. Standard is broad on purpose (volume play). Premium is tight (quality + capacity).

---

## Standard tier (broad — full spectrum)

**Anyone trying to make more, better, or more viral content.**

Both ends of the journey:
- **Beginners** — just starting out, want to learn the system
- **Established business owners ($20–100K/mo)** — already running, need better content engines

**Logic:** every online business owner — wherever they're at — always needs better and more content, because content is what brings in the money. Door is wide open.

**Standard audience desires:**
- More views, better content, learn the system

**Who Standard does NOT serve:**
- Chargeback risks / financially unstable buyers
- "Just looking" tire-kickers

---

## Premium tier (tight — established only)

**$10–40K/mo experts who already have a working offer.** Coaches, agencies, traders, freelancers, info creators with product-market fit who are stuck in 200-view jail and want organic as a real channel.

**No beginners in Premium.** The 1-1 hands-on roadmap requires a working business to scale.

### Premium audience problems
- Stuck in 200-view jail
- No organic revenue / over-reliant on paid
- No editors, no systems
- Inconsistent with content, burnt out
- No clear brand direction or content strategy

### Premium audience desires
- 10x views
- Organic channel that compounds
- Monetization
- Hands-off content team eventually

### Who Premium does NOT serve
- Complete beginners
- Anyone under ~$10K/mo
- Anyone likely to chargeback
