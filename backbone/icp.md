**Anyone trying to make more, better, or more viral content.**

**Who Standard does NOT serve:**

### 3. Biggest problem

They have a real business but no organic content channel. Revenue depends entirely on paid acquisition, referrals, or 1-1 outreach — which means they're trading time/money for every dollar and can't compound. Content is *supposed* to be the leverage, but theirs is stuck under 1K views and converts nothing.

### 4. Top 3 daily frustrations

1. **Posting into the void.** Spending hours making content that gets 200 views, no engagement, zero pipeline impact.
2. **No system, no consistency.** Every piece of content feels like starting from scratch — no repeatable hooks, no editor, no production line. Burnout cycle: post for 2 weeks, ghost for 3.
3. **Watching less-talented people pop.** Seeing creators they consider mid get 100K views and grow paid communities while they grind in obscurity — knowing it's not talent, but unable to crack what those people are doing.

### 5. Moment of highest tension

Posting a video they spent hours on, refreshing the analytics, and watching it die at 180 views — again. The "why am I even doing this" moment. Or: opening Instagram/YouTube and seeing a competitor in their niche hit a viral they know they could've made better.

Secondary tension: the end of a month when paid ad spend ate the margin and they realize organic still contributed nothing.
